This project is a digital 3D model replica of a real life New York apartment interior, meaning that the model was created with the aid of photographs of a real apartment that I had never visited or seen in person. The intention of the work is to look as realistic as possible, while only using basic Maya modeling and texturing techniques. Considering that this was the first model I created and textured in Maya on my own, I am happy with the replication of complex shapes and details of the apartment as well as my ability to find and create high quality textures for those shapes. I did well in paying attention to the details of the photos that were available to me, which allowed me to create a detailed model. There are definitely a few things that could use improvement, though, like adding certain details I omitted from the apartment because I couldnâ€™t replicate them with my skill level at that time, or some of my textures could have been more detailed than they were. Mostly, I feel that the biggest improvement that I could make is simplifying how I went about creating certain shapes in the apartment, over-complicating the construction making it harder for me to adjust and texture them thereafter. In the end it is these things that I wish to improve about the work that effected how I did my future work. I have since improved and hope to improve even more with more practice.
